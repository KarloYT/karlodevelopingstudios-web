What is TheLastGeneration?

TheLastGeneration is a real-life simulation game, which you can play in different modes. Connect with players all over the world in a multiplayer modeor enjoy your self in a singleplayer mode. Compare, Compete, Play, HaveFun. TheLastGeneration is developed by KarloDevelopingStudios and running on Python 3.0. It is also available in Java or C and CS

Is TheLastGeneration free?

Sadly, TheLastGeneration is not available for free, as the game is a premium high-quality game. Also the server's and development costs are high. With these factors applying, we cannot sell/give TheLastGeneration for free.
