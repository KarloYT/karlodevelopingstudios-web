About us | KarloDevelopingStudios


We, as KarloDevelopingStudios are a company that focus on high-quality out-put for a low price. Of course some may think that 50$ for a game is way too much, but once you play it you will understand. We believe that customer feedback is the way to go, that is why you can easily give us feed back at our discord server. On this page, we would like to list some things that we support/stand behind as its important for us to assist/support people that are not accepted well by the public community.

At KarloDevelopingStudios (KDS), we stand firmly against racism in all its forms. We believe in the inherent value and dignity of every individual, regardless of their race, ethnicity, or background. Our company is built on the foundation of inclusivity and equality, and we are committed to creating a safe, respectful, and nurturing environment for everyone. Racism undermines the very essence of humanity, perpetuating unjust disparities and sowing seeds of discord in our communities. We reject racism because it denies people the opportunity to thrive, stifles creativity, and hinders progress. We understand that by embracing diversity, we unlock a wealth of perspectives, ideas, and talents that enrich our collective experiences. Our commitment to eradicating racism is driven by a moral obligation to do what is right, to challenge systemic prejudices, and to break down barriers that have hindered progress for too long. By fostering an anti-racist culture, we empower our employees and customers to engage in open dialogue, educate one another, and work together to build a fairer, more compassionate society.
